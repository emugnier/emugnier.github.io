About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Opera and Sysnet Group at UCSD, working with Prof. Yuanyuan Zhou.

I specialize in practical formal methods for real-world systems, leveraging LLMs to build tools tailored to the user's needs. My previous work focused on access control and security related issues.
